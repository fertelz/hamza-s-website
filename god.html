GOD of War




God of War, released in 2005 by Santa Monica Studio, was a groundbreaking action-adventure game that introduced players to Kratos, a Spartan warrior on a quest for vengeance against the Greek gods.
The game's brutal combat, epic storytelling, and mythological setting quickly made it a standout title for the PlayStation 2, and it became one of the most iconic franchises in gaming history.

In 2018, the series was reimagined with a new entry simply titled God of War.
This time, Kratos is older, wiser, and living in the world of Norse mythology, trying to balance his violent past with his role as a father to his son, Atreus.
The game introduced a more personal narrative, focusing on the father-son relationship and their journey to scatter the ashes of Kratos's wife.

The 2018 God of War was praised for its stunning visuals, seamless combat, and emotional depth, earning numerous Game of the Year awards. It revitalized the franchise with a new perspective on the character of Kratos, making it not only one of PlayStation's best games but also a hallmark of modern action-adventure storytelling.
