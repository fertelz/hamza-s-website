HALO 2




Released in 2004 for the original Xbox, Halo 2 was the highly anticipated sequel to Halo: Combat Evolved, developed by Bungie.
The game continued the story of Master Chief, a super-soldier fighting against the alien Covenant, while introducing players to the Arbiter, an Elite warrior with his own perspective on the war.

Halo 2 revolutionized first-person shooters with its dual-wielding mechanic, improved AI, and cinematic storytelling.
However, its biggest impact came from its online multiplayer, which was a defining moment for Xbox Live.
The introduction of matchmaking and competitive ranked play set the foundation for modern online gaming.

Despite its infamous cliffhanger ending, Halo 2 became one of the best-selling Xbox games of all time and solidified Halo as Microsoft's flagship franchise.
Its legacy lives on through Halo: The Master Chief Collection, where fans can experience its iconic campaign and multiplayer once again.
